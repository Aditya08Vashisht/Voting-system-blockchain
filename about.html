About Us

About Us

A decentralised Online Voting System using blockchain system on solidity. .

We are building a decentralised blockchain system on solidity. a front-end website, backend done in javascript, and logic of blockchain in solidity. through the blockchain, we are trying to create a voting system that will let users vote independently and securely, The authentication will be done through node js, and server in express js with a connection through data base, After successful authentication, the user will be forwarded to solidity module where the secure voting will be done. after the voting, a block will be created on the blockchain.

Why Online Vote

To Make Sure Every Vote Counts

Equality and Representation

Ensuring that every vote counts is not just a matter of democratic principle; it's also a matter of equity and representation. Every community, regardless of its size or demographic makeup, deserves a say in its governance.

Impact Beyond Booth

The impact of our votes extends far beyond the confines of the voting booth. Elected officials make decisions that affect every aspect of our lives – from education and healthcare to infrastructure and the environment. When we exercise our right to vote, we have a say in who will represent our interests and make these crucial decisions on our behalf.

Democracy at Its Core

At the core of democracy is the belief that every citizen's voice matters. Regardless of background, creed, or circumstance, each person has a right to be heard. When we cast our votes, we exercise this right and contribute to the collective decision-making process that guides our society.
